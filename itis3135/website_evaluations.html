Website Evaluations

Good Website - Apple

Apple's website is a great example of clean, modern design. It's visually appealing, easy to navigate, and follows best practices for web design.

  • Contrast: The contrast between text and background is excellent, making everything easy to read and distinguish.
  • Repetition: Consistent design elements are used throughout the site, creating a cohesive look and brand identity.
  • Alignment: Everything is neatly aligned, giving the site a polished and professional feel while also enhancing readability.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped together, making it easy for users to find what they need quickly, creating a good UX.
