What is Photography?

Photography is the art of capturing light with a camera to create images, allowing us to freeze moments in time and express creativity through composition, lighting, and subject matter.

Who Am I?

I'm Cameron Bentley, a passionate photographer capturing the beauty of the world, with particular interest in landscape, portrait, and storytelling photography

Best Times for Photography

The best times for photography are during the golden hours—shortly after sunrise and before sunset—when the light is soft and warm.

Best Locations

Some of the best photography locations include national and state parks, urban landscapes, and coastal areas, each offering unique opportunities for capturing stunning shots.

How to Get Started

To get started with photography, all you need is a camera, a keen eye for composition, and a willingness to experiment with different techniques while expressing your own unique perspective.

Why I Love Photography

Photography lets me express my creativity, capture meaningful moments, and share my unique perspective with the world. I find it to be a deeply fulfilling and rewarding passion.
